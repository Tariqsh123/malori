A Story of Pain, Faith & Restoration

By Nana

Skeletons and Light: Finding God in the Dark Places is a deeply moving true story of pain, survival, and divine restoration. From the shadows of childhood trauma, confusion, and emotional brokenness, Nana takes readers on a journey through some of the darkest seasons of her life.

Growing up surrounded by fear, unanswered questions, and spiritual battles she could not understand, she learns what it means to endure when life feels overwhelming. Yet even in the most painful moments, God was present — quietly working, protecting, and leading her toward healing.

This is more than a story of suffering. It is a testimony of hope, faith, and the power of God to bring light into the darkest places. Through honesty and vulnerability, Nana shows that the wounds we hide, the pain we survive, and the battles we fight can all become part of a greater purpose.

If you have ever felt forgotten, broken, or unsure whether God still sees you, this book will remind you that He does. And even in the dark places, His light still shines.
